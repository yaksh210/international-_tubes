About Us

Welcome to International Tubes

Established in 2011, International Tubes India Pvt. Ltd. has emerged as a Leading Precision Tube Supplier in the industry. Founded with a vision to redefine precision tubing in the automotive sector, we have since expanded our operations to cater to a wide range of industries, including automotive, ABC, and industrial tubing. As part of the Kanunga Group of companies, we are driven by a passion for excellence and innovation, continuously striving to push the boundaries of what's possible in precision engineering.

Driving Precision, Shaping the Future:

At ITIPL, we are more than just manufacturers; we are architects of precision, architects of the future. Our commitment to excellence, combined with our relentless pursuit of innovation, sets us apart as a leader in the industry. We are dedicated to driving precision in everything we do and shaping the future of precision tube engineering.
